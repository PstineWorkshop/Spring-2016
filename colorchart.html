The Color Gold

Gold causes happiness and because it implies you have wealth and are experiencing success. Gold is used for victory success or having an abundance of something, most people strive for making this color very popular for statues, trophies etc. gold tend to make people happy. Some even become compassionate and uses their wealth for others. It also has made people greedy. Gold can also make people feel better than others and often act snobby toward others. Gold generally cause the feelings of generosity, compassion and enlightenment. Gold is warm color.

"Gold evokes the feeling of prestige. The meaning of gold is illumination, wisdom, and wealth. Gold often symbolizes high quality."

Like every gold has its place in world culture and means different things to different people. But the main ideas are constant though. North American religion view gold and indicator of wealth. In China gold represents the earth. Most other countries view gold as way to measure wealth, success etc. gold in psychology represent male masculinity. It is also means generosity, happiness, tradition brilliance in psychology as well.
